Score Member Type Description Recommended Next Steps
0 Explorers Explorers have joined but not yet attended an event. Design an event specifically designed for new members or help them explore the group via the blog or facebook group.
0-5 New Member New members have attended a few events but do not yet experience the community as a major part of their identity. Help new members
1) Connect with other members who share their interests.
2) Get into a rhythm of attending events.
5-10 Active Member Active members feel a sense of connection and identity with their community. They attend event regularly Help active members find a way to contribute to the community as a volunteer, member of the Open Heart Brigade, or Event Host.
10-20 Contributing Member Contributing Members are actively engaged in co-creating the community. Make sure to regularly communicate vision and changes to contributing members. These members will often step into leadership without prompting if they can see where the community is headed.
20+ Leadership The leadership team sets the vision and facilitates other members engagement. Look for opportunities to support leaderships development and to energize leadership to compensate for burn out.
