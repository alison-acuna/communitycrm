Douglas Atkin, expert in community management, popularized the Commitment Curve model for how "to ramp up people from low-barrier-to-entry actions to increasingly difficult ones." The idea is to move members towards greater engagement and ensures that a community manager is making appropriate asks of members. Addtionally, "lower investment actions tend to predispose people to make higher investment actions because they literally become more invested in the community".


The Community CRM is a tool designed to support community managers in encouraging their members to take the next step on the community commitment curve. The Community CRM tracks member engagement through a member engagement score and tracks member interests.

The Community CRM is designed for community managers, religious leaders, and non-profit with a community component.
